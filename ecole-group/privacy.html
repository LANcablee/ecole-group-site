Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how Ecole Group Ltd (“we”, “us”) handles personal information in connection with this website. We are committed to handling your data openly and lawfully.

Who we are

Ecole Group Ltd is a distribution company registered in the United Kingdom. For any privacy question, contact us at info@ecolegroup.co.uk. [Registered office address and company number to be added.]

What we collect

This website has no contact forms, accounts or logins. We only receive personal information if you choose to email us — in which case we receive your email address and whatever you include in your message.

As with most websites, our hosting and security provider may automatically process limited technical data (such as an IP address) to keep the site secure and running. This is not used to identify you for marketing.

Why we use it

We use the information you send us solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to take steps at your request. Our lawful bases under UK GDPR are your consent in choosing to contact us and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may rely on trusted service providers (for example, our email and website hosting providers) who process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry correspondence only for as long as necessary to deal with your request and for our legitimate business records, after which it is deleted.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have rights including access, correction, deletion and objection to processing. To exercise these, email info@ecolegroup.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
