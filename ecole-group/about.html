About

Ecole Group is an independent distribution company based in the United Kingdom.

We identify brands of genuine quality — often from beyond the UK — and give them a considered route to the British market.

We are deliberately selective. Rather than carry many lines, we take on a small number of brands and commit to each one fully: dependable supply, presentation that respects the product, and a close working relationship with the retailers who stock it.

Our first brand is ELY’S CURE, a natural skincare range from Turkey. In time the group will extend into further categories — beginning with textiles, and, in the longer term, the chemicals sector. Each will be introduced only when it can be done to the same standard.
